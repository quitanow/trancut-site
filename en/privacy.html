Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TranCut collects, uses, and protects information through the TranCut website.

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the TranCut website, including its English, Traditional Chinese, and Simplified Chinese pages, and to messages you send to us through our listed support email.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you choose to send us, such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message.
  • Basic technical information that may be collected automatically when you visit the website, such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring pages, and general usage logs.
  • Information needed to maintain site security, troubleshoot issues, and improve website performance.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Respond to support requests, product questions, and general inquiries.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website.
  • Monitor website reliability, security, and performance.
  • Communicate with you about your inquiry or request.

4. What We Do Not Currently Collect Through Public Website Features

At this stage, TranCut’s public website does not publicly provide a full online video upload and processing service. If that changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before broader public rollout.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information through this website. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the website, email, hosting, security, or related technical services, subject to appropriate operational needs.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect our rights, users, or services.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The website may use basic technical tools, logs, or similar technologies to support site functionality, security, and performance. If we add analytics, account systems, or more advanced tracking tools in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy as appropriate.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for support, security, operational, and legal purposes. We may delete or anonymize information when it is no longer reasonably needed.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. International Visitors

TranCut may be accessed by users in different countries and regions. By using the website, you understand that information may be processed in locations where we or our service providers operate.

10. Children’s Privacy

The website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 through the public website.

11. Your Choices

You may choose not to send us personal information through email or other direct contact methods. If you have a privacy-related question or request regarding information you previously shared with us, please contact us using the email below.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please contact: support@trancut.com
